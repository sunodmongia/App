SaaS Privacy Policy: Complete Guide to Compliance and Trust

By Wire Team • August 8, 2025 • 16 min read

Welcome—this privacy policy outlines how SaaSApp collects, uses, and safeguards your personal data.

🔍 Scope

This policy covers data collected through our platform, website, apps, APIs, and offline channels.

📊 Information We Collect

1.1 Provided by You

  • Account info: contact details, company, role, avatars.
  • Communications: messages, surveys, and support requests.
  • Billing: payment data (processed securely by third parties).

1.2 Automatically Collected

  • Device & browser info: IP, OS, screen size, usage events.
  • Cookies and tracking (analytics, ads, personalization).

1.3 From Third Parties

We may combine data from partners, social networks, and public sources for enhanced services.

⚡ How We Use Your Data

  • Operate and improve our platform and services.
  • Manage subscriptions and process payments.
  • Personalize content and communications.
  • Detect threats, abuse, and fraud prevention.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our rights.

🛡️ Legal Bases

Depending on region: consent, contractual necessity, legitimate interests, or compliance with laws.

🍪 Cookies

  • Essential: login and core functions
  • Analytics: user behavior and site performance
  • Marketing: with your consent only

You can adjust these anytime in your browser or via our platform settings.

🔗 Sharing & Disclosure

  • Service providers: hosting, analytics, payments, communications.
  • Legal: compliance, fraud prevention, legal processes.
  • Business transactions: mergers, acquisitions with notice.

We do not sell your personal information.

🗄️ Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary for operations, legal requirements, or your instructions.

🔒 Security

  • Encryption at rest and in transit
  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Regular audits and vulnerability scanning
  • Incident reporting via sunodmongia2003@gmail.com

🌎 International Transfers

Your data may be transferred outside your country with appropriate safeguards.

👶 Children's Privacy

The platform is intended for users aged 13+. We do not knowingly collect data from children below this age.

📝 Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • Receive data portability

Contact us at sunodmongia2003@gmail.com to exercise your rights.
